Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to sell?
Listing items is completely free on Biashara Connect. We only charge a small commission when you successfully make a sale through our platform.
How do I receive payments?
We support M-Pesa, cash on delivery, and bank transfers. Most sellers prefer M-Pesa for instant payments.
How long do listings stay active?
Listings remain active for 30 days. You can renew them for free at any time before they expire.
Can I edit my listing after posting?
Yes, you can edit your listing anytime from your seller dashboard to update prices, photos, or descriptions.
Is my personal information safe?
We never share your phone number or personal details publicly. We use a secure messaging system to connect buyers and sellers.
