Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list a product for sale? +
To list a product, go to the "Sell on Biashara" page, click "List a Product", and fill out the product details form. You'll need to provide clear photos, a detailed description, pricing, and shipping information. Once submitted, our team will review your listing (usually within 24 hours) before it goes live.
What are the seller fees on Biashara? +
Biashara charges a 10% commission on each successful sale. This fee covers payment processing, customer support, and platform maintenance. There are no listing fees or monthly subscriptions for basic accounts. Premium seller accounts ($19.99/month) receive reduced commission rates and additional features.
How do I handle returns and refunds? +
Sellers can set their own return policies in their account settings. Biashara recommends offering at least a 14-day return window. When a return is requested, you'll be notified and can accept or decline based on your policy. If you accept, we provide a prepaid return label. Once the item is received and verified, the refund is processed automatically.
How can I promote my listings? +
You can promote your listings through our advertising platform. Go to "Insights" and select "Promotions" to create targeted campaigns. You can also improve visibility by optimizing your listings with relevant keywords, high-quality images, and competitive pricing. Premium sellers get featured placement in search results.
When do I get paid for my sales? +
Payments are processed 2 business days after an order is marked as delivered. Funds are deposited directly to your verified bank account or PayPal. You can track all payments in the "Earnings" section of your dashboard. For new sellers, there may be a 7-day holding period for the first few transactions.
